Welcome to Sweet Bites, where passion meets flavor in every bite!

Our cookies are filled with rich, creamy brigadeiro, bringing a taste of Brazil to your home. Created by a Brazilian baker with a love for sweet treats, each cookie is a masterpiece of indulgence.

Our Journey

Our founder, Tania Mara Marcos, grew up in a household where baking was a cherished tradition. Inspired by her family's recipes and the rich flavors of Brazil, she set out to create cookies that would bring joy and delight to everyone who tasted them. Today, Sweet Bites continues this tradition, combining the authentic taste of Brazilian brigadeiro with modern baking techniques.

Our Mission

At Sweet Bites, our mission is simple: to bring happiness to our customers, one bite at a time. We believe in celebrating life's small moments and creating memories through our delicious cookies. Every cookie we make is a testament to our love for baking and our commitment to quality.

Red Velvet Cheesecake

Treat yourself to our rich red velvet cookie dough, loaded with smooth white Belgian chocolate chips and filled with a creamy, homemade cheesecake center.It's like biting into a slice of heaven.

Milky Way

Enjoy our dark cacao cookie dough, filled with a creamy milk filling and topped with Nido milk powder. Each bite is a chocolatey, creamy delight that melts in your mouth.

Brigadeiro Dream

Dive into our classic cookie dough, packed with crunchy chocolate pieces and milk Belgian chocolate, all surrounding a rich, handmade brigadeiro filling. It's a taste of Brazil in every bite.

Coconut Paradise

Enjoy the perfect blend of flavors with our chocolate cookie dough, filled with smooth white Belgian chocolate chips and a creamy coconut center. It's a tropical escape in a cookie.

Churros

Experience the warmth of our cinnamon churros cookie dough, filled with gooey milk Belgian chocolate and the best dulce de leche. It's like a hug in cookie form.
